Our Story

Where Tokyo
meets Berlin

Nestled in the heart of Berlin Mitte's vibrant Scheunenviertel quarter, Iimori Ramen brings the soul of Japanese ramen culture to the cobblestone streets of Berlin. We believe that a great bowl of ramen is not just a meal — it is a ritual.

Every broth is simmered for 18 hours. Every noodle is pulled fresh each morning. Our ingredients are sourced directly from Japanese producers and trusted Berlin-based farms, creating a bridge between two food cultures we love deeply.
